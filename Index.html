Installation

Download the CloudNest installer from the official website. Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions to complete setup.

Usage

After installation, launch CloudNest from your applications menu. Sign in with your account to start managing your cloud resources.

Troubleshooting

Problem: Application fails to start

Ensure that your system meets the minimum requirements. Try reinstalling the application if the issue persists.

Problem: Unable to sync data

Check your internet connection and verify that your login credentials are correct. Restart the application and try syncing again.
